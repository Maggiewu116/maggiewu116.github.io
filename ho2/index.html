Company taxi visualization and abnormal analysis with Power Bi and SQL

The company's taxi platform has tens of thousands of taxi orders per year. Via Power Bi associating with the internal organizational structure, in order to facilitate the continuous review of the compliance of the company's taxi usage, and new business monitoring matrix are designed via Dax function to realize automatic calculation from overall perspective or regional perspective.

Customer life cycle analysis and prediction with R

Based on a company's auto insurance customer retention rate, customers are divided into three groups according to their profit margins. Long-term data shows that customers may give up renewal in the twelfth year due to certain events, such as marriage, childbirth or college. Therefore, considering the limited data at hand, try to use shifted beta geometric distribution (sBG method) to predict the lifetime value of potential customers within 12 years.

Text analysis of product reviews on Twitter & other projects, such as RPA and SQL
